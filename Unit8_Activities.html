UNIT 8 · ACTIVITY

Pitch Theatre

3 × 90 min · Cohort-based coachingThe Art of Pitching

Objective

Move a rough idea through Bostelaar's Distill → Decompose → Prioritise pipeline, then deliver it live under time pressure to a mock investor panel.

Materials

  • Core Message worksheet
  • StoryBrand Arc canvas
  • Kawasaki 10-slide template
  • Pitch rehearsal rubric
  • Video recording setup

Steps

Step 1 · 30 min · Session 1
Distill

One-sentence pitch: 'We help [X] to [Y] by [Z].' Write 5 versions. Pick the one that survives the most scepticism.

Step 2 · 30 min · Session 1
Decompose

Break the pitch into 7 StoryBrand elements. Which element is weakest?

Step 3 · 30 min · Session 1
Prioritise

Rank the elements by audience relevance. Identify the single most-important beat the pitch must land.

Step 4 · Homework
Deck Build

Build the 10 Kawasaki slides: Problem, Solution, Business Model, Magic, GTM, Competition, Team, Financials, Status, Timeline.

Step 5 · Session 2
Rehearsal Round 1

3-minute pitch to peers. Record. Receive one 'clearer' note and one 'sharper' note.

Step 6 · Session 2
Rehearsal Round 2

Incorporate notes. Cut 30 seconds. Re-record.

Step 7 · Session 3
Live Pitch

3-minute pitch to mock investor panel. 2-minute Q&A. Panel votes: fund / kill / send back.

Deliverable. Final pitch deck (PDF) + 3-minute video recording + 300-word reflection on what the Distill-Decompose-Prioritise pipeline changed.

Rubric

Knowledge & Understanding 20% · Application 30% · Critical Thinking 25% · Communication 15% · AI Use 10%.
