Meadows' Leverage Points

5 Frameworks

Donella Meadows' canonical 1999 paper — twelve places to intervene in a system, ranked from weakest (adjusting parameters) to strongest (transcending paradigms).

12 Leverage Points

Parameters · Buffers · Stocks & Flows · Delays · Loops · Info · Rules · Self-org · Goals · Paradigm · Transcending.
