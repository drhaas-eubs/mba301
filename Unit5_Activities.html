UNIT 5 · ACTIVITY

Opportunity Scan

Multi-session · Homework + in-class synthesisDeveloping Successful Business Ideas

Objective

Run a real JTBD discovery cycle: interview 5 potential customers, derive functional/emotional/social jobs, and propose 3 testable hypotheses for Problem-Solution Fit.

Materials

  • JTBD interview guide
  • Switch interview worksheet
  • Lean Canvas template
  • Experiment design sheet

Steps

Step 1 · Pre-session
Pick a Domain

Choose a domain where you observe friction (commuting, meal planning, language learning, etc.).

Step 2 · Homework · 30 min each
5 Switch Interviews

Find 5 people who recently switched solutions in that domain. Ask: what prompted the switch? Use the 4 Forces (Push / Pull / Anxiety / Habit).

Step 3 · 30 min in class
JTBD Synthesis

Cluster findings into functional, emotional, and social jobs. Identify the top 'unmet outcome' across your interviews.

Step 4 · 30 min
Lean Canvas

Build a Lean Canvas grounded in interview data. The Problem block is the sharpest JTBD you uncovered.

Step 5 · 20 min
3 Experiments for PSF

Propose 3 Riskiest Assumption Tests — concierge, fake-door, smoke-test — ranked by learning velocity.

Step 6 · 10 min
Peer Review

Swap canvases with another team. Identify the weakest evidence block and suggest a test.

Deliverable. Interview transcripts (anonymised) + Lean Canvas + 3-experiment plan + 500-word PSF brief. PDF upload.

Rubric

Knowledge & Understanding 20% · Application 30% · Critical Thinking 25% · Communication 15% · AI Use 10%.
