UNIT 1 · ACTIVITY

Empathy Workshop

90 min · Individual → Pair → PlenaryDesign Thinking as Mindset

Objective

Develop field-ready empathy research skills by conducting, capturing, and synthesising user interviews into actionable insights.

Materials

  • Empathy map canvas (A3)
  • Stakeholder map canvas (A3)
  • Customer journey canvas (A3)
  • Interview guide (provided)
  • Sticky notes, markers

Steps

Step 1 · 20 min · Pair
Observation & Interview

Pair up. Interview partner about their last frustrating service experience. Open with: 'Tell me about a time when…' Probe with 5 Whys.

Step 2 · 15 min · Individual
Empathy Map

Fill the Says / Thinks / Does / Feels quadrants based on the interview. Quote directly where you can; infer where you can't.

Step 3 · 15 min · Individual
Stakeholder Map

Map the actors around your partner's experience. Identify primary, secondary, and tertiary stakeholders. Which have power? Which have pain?

Step 4 · 20 min · Individual
Journey Map

Plot your partner's journey: awareness → decision → experience → aftermath. Mark emotional peaks and valleys.

Step 5 · 10 min · Pair
Insight Synthesis

Share maps. Co-create 3 How Might We… questions that reframe the sharpest pain into an actionable prompt.

Step 6 · 10 min · Whole room
Plenary

Three teams share their sharpest HMW. Room votes on which reframes open the most territory.

Deliverable. Upload all three completed canvases (empathy / stakeholder / journey) and three HMW questions to the course platform by end of session.

Rubric

Knowledge & Understanding 20% · Application 30% · Critical Thinking 25% · Communication 15% · AI Use 10%.
