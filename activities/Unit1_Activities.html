Empathy Workshop

Design Thinking as Mindset

© 2026 Dr. H. Haas · EU Business School

FormatEmpathy Workshop
Duration90 minutes
GroupingPairs, then teams of 4

Brief

A field-ready empathy map, journey map and stakeholder map sprint.

Learning Objectives

  • Practise non-leading interviewing technique (the observe-vs-infer discipline).
  • Translate raw quotes into user insights via the Empathy Map canvas.
  • Trace an end-to-end user experience through a journey map with emotion line.

Materials

  • A3 Empathy Map templates (one per pair)
  • A3 Journey Map templates
  • Sticky notes in 4 colours
  • Fine markers
  • Timer

Timing & Phases

0–15 min · Framing
Introduce the HCD 3 Lenses (Desirability × Feasibility × Viability). Clarify the design challenge: How might we redesign the first-year MBA onboarding experience?
15–40 min · Interview Practice (Pairs)
Round-robin interviews, 10 min each direction, using open-ended prompts only. Partner captures verbatim quotes — no paraphrasing.
40–65 min · Empathy Map Build
Each pair fills in the Says · Thinks · Does · Feels canvas from the raw notes. Highlight contradictions (what they said vs what they did) — these are gold.
65–80 min · Journey Map
Plot the onboarding experience as a timeline with an emotion line above. Mark pain points and moments of delight.
80–90 min · Debrief
Each team surfaces one insight that surprised them and one Point-of-View statement in the form [user] needs [need] because [insight].

Deliverable

Required output

One completed Empathy Map + Journey Map per pair, plus a single-sentence POV statement ready to seed Unit 2 Ideation.
