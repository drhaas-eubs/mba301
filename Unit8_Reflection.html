Reflection Prompts

Prompt 1

One-sentence pitch — Write your own one-sentence pitch using 'we help X to Y by Z'. Ask 3 people to repeat it 24 hours later. Count the hits.

Prompt 2

StoryBrand beats — In your most recent important presentation, which of Miller's 7 elements was weakest? Rewrite that slide.

Prompt 3

Kawasaki discipline — Take your last deck and cut it to 10 slides. What did you lose? What did you gain?
