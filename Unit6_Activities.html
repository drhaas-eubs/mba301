UNIT 6 · ACTIVITY

Foundry Simulation

120 min · Teams of 3–4Moving from an Idea to an Entrepreneurial Firm

Objective

Move a validated idea through the four operational decisions every early-stage founder must make: legal form, cap table, runway, and first hire.

Materials

  • Foundry Dashboard (provided)
  • Cap table spreadsheet
  • Runway calculator
  • Hiring framework cards
  • Scenario deck

Steps

Step 1 · 10 min
Pick a Scenario

Draw a scenario card: early-stage SaaS, hardware, consumer, or B2B services. Each has different cash flow and regulatory constraints.

Step 2 · 15 min
Legal Form Decision

Team picks legal form (GmbH, Ltd, LLC, sole trader). Defend choice on liability, tax, and capital-raising grounds.

Step 3 · 25 min
Cap Table v1

3 co-founders: set equity split. Create option pool. Model what happens after seed round at €2M post-money for €500K.

Step 4 · 20 min
Runway & Burn

Build a 12-month cash model. Salaries, cloud, legal, tools. What's the burn? When does runway end?

Step 5 · 20 min
First Hire Brief

Pick the first non-founder hire. Write the role brief. Defend why this role, not another.

Step 6 · 30 min · Plenary
Foundry Review

Each team presents its Foundry Dashboard. Faculty + peers challenge one of the four decisions. Team defends or revises.

Deliverable. Foundry Dashboard (single PDF, 4 pages) with legal form, cap table, runway model, and first-hire brief.

Rubric

Knowledge & Understanding 20% · Application 30% · Critical Thinking 25% · Communication 15% · AI Use 10%.
