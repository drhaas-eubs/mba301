UNIT 2 · ACTIVITY

Creative Sprint

90 min · Small teams of 4–5Ideation

Objective

Execute a compressed diverge-converge cycle on a real brief: generate 30+ ideas, filter them rigorously, and leave the room with one experiment worth running.

Materials

  • Brief card (provided)
  • Crazy 8s template
  • Dot-voting stickers
  • Impact–Effort matrix (A3)
  • NUF scoring sheet

Steps

Step 1 · 10 min
Brief & Warm-up

Read the brief out loud as a team. Reformulate as a How Might We question. Warm up with 3 'worst possible ideas' to break judgement reflexes.

Step 2 · 10 min · Individual
Crazy 8s

Each team member: 8 ideas, 8 minutes, one per folded panel. No talking. No judging. Quantity over quality.

Step 3 · 15 min · Team
Share & Brainwriting 6-3-5

3 minutes per round, silent, build on each other's ideas. Five rounds.

Step 4 · 5 min
Silent Dot Voting

Every member gets 5 dots. Distribute across the wall. Clusters emerge naturally.

Step 5 · 15 min
Convergence · NUF Test

Take top 5 ideas by dots. Score each 1–10 on Novel, Useful, Feasible. Rank by total.

Step 6 · 15 min
Impact–Effort Plot

Plot top 5 on Impact × Effort matrix. Pick one from the 'high impact, low effort' quadrant to prototype next.

Step 7 · 20 min · Plenary
Pitch-Back

Each team pitches its chosen experiment in 90 seconds. Room gives one 'yes, and…' and one 'but what about…'.

Deliverable. Photo of the Impact–Effort matrix + 90-second video pitch of the chosen experiment, uploaded to course platform.

Rubric

Knowledge & Understanding 20% · Application 30% · Critical Thinking 25% · Communication 15% · AI Use 10%.
