UNIT 7 · ACTIVITY

Corporate Venture Lab

10-week module · Integrated capstoneIntrapreneurship

Objective

Design, staff, and run a miniature 10-week corporate accelerator for a real (or provided) parent organisation — from sponsor pitch to demo day.

Materials

  • Parent-org brief
  • Sponsor prospectus template
  • Gate-review checklist
  • Demo-day rubric
  • Internal term sheet template

Steps

Step 1 · Week 1
Parent-Org Scoping

Understand parent's strategic goals, risk appetite, and innovation gaps. Interview 2 senior stakeholders.

Step 2 · Week 2
Sponsor Prospectus

Draft the sponsor prospectus: why this lab, what success looks like, what gates mean kill. Pitch to faculty-as-sponsor.

Step 3 · Week 3
Team Recruitment

Form 3-person teams (founder, engineer, designer profile). Assign first brief.

Step 4 · Weeks 4–5
Sprint 1 (Discovery)

5–10 customer interviews, Problem-Solution Fit assessment. Gate review 1.

Step 5 · Weeks 6–7
Sprint 2 (Validation)

MVP built, 3 experiments run, metrics captured. Gate review 2.

Step 6 · Weeks 8–9
Sprint 3 (Traction)

Scale the highest-signal experiment. Show one traction metric moving. Final gate.

Step 7 · Week 10
Demo Day

6-minute pitch + 4-minute Q&A to sponsor + external panel. Venture is continued, transferred to parent, or closed.

Deliverable. Sponsor prospectus (wk 2) + 3 gate-review memos (wks 5, 7, 9) + final demo-day pitch deck + internal term sheet proposal.

Rubric

Knowledge & Understanding 20% · Application 30% · Critical Thinking 25% · Communication 15% · AI Use 10%.
