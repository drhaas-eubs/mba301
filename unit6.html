Unit Economics

5 Frameworks

The five numbers that determine whether a venture's growth is creating or destroying value. If unit economics don't work at N customers, they won't work at 10N.

CAC

Customer Acquisition Cost = total sales & marketing spend ÷ new customers acquired.

Slibrary 18

LTV

Customer Lifetime Value = ARPU × gross margin × average customer lifetime.

Slibrary 18

LTV/CAC Ratio

Rule of thumb: ≥ 3. Below 1 means you lose money on every customer.

Slibrary 18

Payback Period

Months of gross margin it takes to earn back CAC. SaaS benchmark: < 12 months.

Slibrary 18

Contribution Margin & Burn

CM per unit × volume − fixed costs = profit. Burn = fixed − contribution.
