Effectuation Principles

5 Frameworks

Sarasvathy's five principles drawn from 27 expert entrepreneurs. Effectual logic — what to do when you can't predict the future — is the most-taught entrepreneurship framework of the last 25 years.

Bird-in-Hand

Start with who you are, what you know, whom you know. Means over ends.

Uncertainty & Risk

5 Frameworks

Five frameworks for distinguishing situations you can model from situations you cannot — and for acting responsibly in both. Furr's Possibility Quadrant is the centrepiece.

Knight's Risk vs Uncertainty

Risk: probabilities known. Uncertainty: unknowable. Different tools for each.

Slibrary 11

Possibility Quadrant

Furr's 2×2 — map your uncertainty by certainty of cause and certainty of outcome.

Slibrary 11
