UNIT 7 · PRE-READING

Intrapreneurship — Empower Your Employees

WhatAVenture (eds.)WhatAVenture Report · 2021
Key framework. Building Blocks of Corporate Entrepreneurship

Key Takeaways

  1. Intrapreneurship succeeds or fails on 6 building blocks: sponsorship, talent, capital, gates, autonomy, and exit paths.
  2. Autonomy without gates produces entertainment; gates without autonomy produce compliance. Both kill ventures.
  3. The sponsor-engineer pairing (senior political cover + founder-operator) is the dominant pattern in successful corporate accelerators.
  4. Kill criteria must be pre-declared in the sponsor prospectus — political kills destroy trust in the system.
  5. Parent organisations often forget to design the exit path — the handback to a BU or the spin-out — and kill good ventures at the finish line.

Discussion Questions

  1. Which of the 6 building blocks is weakest at your organisation? What specifically is missing?
  2. Propose 3 kill criteria that would be acceptable to both founders and sponsors. What makes them objective?
  3. Design the exit path for a 10-week accelerator venture — what are the options at Demo Day?
