UNIT 4 · PRE-READING

The Upside of Uncertainty

Nathan Furr & Susannah Harmon FurrHarvard Business Review Press · 2022
Key framework. The Possibility Quadrant · 5 Principles of Effectual Action

Key Takeaways

  1. Uncertainty is not the absence of information — it is the presence of possibility.
  2. The Possibility Quadrant distinguishes between certainty of cause and certainty of outcome — different quadrants call for different tools.
  3. Effectuation's 5 principles (bird-in-hand, affordable loss, lemonade, crazy quilt, pilot-in-the-plane) are the tested responses to each quadrant.
  4. The risks of inaction are systematically under-weighted in education — the costs of missed upside are invisible.
  5. Framing uncertainty as possibility — rather than threat — is the most predictive cognitive move in successful entrepreneurs.

Discussion Questions

  1. Locate your current professional uncertainty on the Possibility Quadrant. Which quadrant is it in?
  2. Which effectuation principle do you under-use most in your work?
  3. What is the 'cost of inaction' you are currently paying — and is it visible on your balance sheet?
Furr, N., & Furr, S.H. (2022). The Upside of Uncertainty: A Guide to Finding Possibility in the Unknown. HBR Press.
