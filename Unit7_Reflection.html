Independent study300–500 words · submit before next session

Reflect on the following three questions. Submit a 300–500 word combined response via the course platform before the next session. Format: PDF, single-spaced, with your name and the unit number in the header.

Reflection Prompts

Prompt 1

Sponsor-engineer — Map the sponsor-engineer pairs at your current organisation. Which pairs are missing a sponsor? Which are missing an engineer?

Prompt 2

Kill criteria — Draft 3 kill criteria for a real project at your organisation. Would the sponsor agree to them in writing?

Prompt 3

Exit path — For a venture you know, describe the exit path at Demo Day. Who owns it? Who pays? Who staffs it?

Submission. PDF · 300–500 words · single-spaced · header with your name + MBA301 Unit 7 Reflection · due before next session on the course platform.
